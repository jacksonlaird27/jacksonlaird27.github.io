About Site

This is a stan website dedicated to the immaculate British pop singer known as Charli XCX.

Charli XCX has ushered in a new era of electronic pop music with the help of visionary artists and producers like PC Music's A.G. Cook and Grammy-nominated icon SOPHIE. Since the 2016 release of her game-changing EP "Vroom Vroom," Charli's status as the future of pop music remains entirely uncontested. She is beloved for her experimental melodies, intelligent lyrics, energetic stage presence, and tireless advocacy for LGBTQ rights. Those who disagree with any of this are asked to kindly remove themselves from this webpage and vigorously clear any potential blockage in their ear canal using a Q-tip.

Please utilize the links below to explore Charli's iconic discography* and peruse her stylish (albeit overpriced) merchandise.

Discography

True Romance (2013)


YouTube Spotify Apple Music

Charli's first major release following two free-to-download mixtapes, True Romance showcases a softer XCX sound with a subtle 80's influence.

Recommended songs: Grins, So Far Away, Black Roses

Vroom Vroom - EP (2016)


YouTube Spotify Apple Music

This EP is the product of Charli's first collaborations with UK experimental group PC Music, and was the launchpad for her to fully realize her potential to change pop music forever.

Recommended songs: Vroom Vroom, Trophy

Number 1 Angel (2017)


YouTube Spotify Apple Music

Number 1 Angel was the first of two ten-track mixtapes released by Charli in 2017. It expanded on the vision of Vroom Vroom while incorporating the influences of its many features and collaborators, making for an unforgettable listener experience.

Recommended songs: Dreamer, Roll With me, White Roses

Pop 2 (2017)


YouTube Spotify Apple Music

Pop 2 marked Charli's most experimental venture yet, and she managed to craft a mixtape that is both mechanically harsh and stunningly beautiful.

Recommended songs: Backseat, Unlock It, Track 10

Charli (2019)


YouTube Spotify Apple Music

Charli's self-titled album dropped after years of her releasing smaller projects in lieu of a full-length album. The music speaks for itself, and it was well worth the wait.

Recommended songs: Gone, Click, Silver Cross

how i'm feeling now (2020)


YouTube Spotify Apple Music

Conceived, recorded and released in spring 2020, how i'm feeling now is a time capsule that explores the anxieties of the early days of the COVID-19 pandemic through Charli's unique musical lens.

Recommended songs: detonate, anthems, visions




*Certain misguided souls may attempt to tell you that Charli XCX also released an album in 2014 called "Sucker." However, we here at the Charli XCX Stan Site choose to believe that no such album exists.
